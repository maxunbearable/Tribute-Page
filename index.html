Best Marcus Aurelius quotes

  1. The happiness of your life depends upon the quality of your thoughts: therefore, guard accordingly, and take care that you entertain no notions unsuitable to virtue and reasonable nature.
  2. When you arise in the morning, think of what a precious privilege it is to be alive - to breathe, to think, to enjoy, to love.
  3. The object of life is not to be on the side of the majority, but to escape finding oneself in the ranks of the insane.
  4. It is not death that a man should fear, but he should fear never beginning to live.
  5. Very little is needed to make a happy life; it is all within yourself, in your way of thinking.
  6. Accept the things to which fate binds you, and love the people with whom fate brings you together, but do so with all your heart.
  7. You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength.
  8. Reject your sense of injury and the injury itself disappears.
  9. Never let the future disturb you. You will meet it, if you have to, with the same weapons of reason which today arm you against the present.
  10. Waste no more time arguing about what a good man should be. Be one.
